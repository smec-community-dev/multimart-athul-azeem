Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for delivery within 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Some restrictions may apply.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also track it from your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, UPI, and net banking. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, we have multiple retail locations. Visit our store locator page to find the nearest one to you.
