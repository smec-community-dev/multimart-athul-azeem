Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Some items like electronics may have different return windows.

You can change your delivery address before your order ships by contacting customer support. Once shipped, address changes may not be possible.

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping options to other countries in the future.

Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery options are available for faster shipping (1-3 business days).

You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled.

You can contact us via email at support@shophub.com or call us at +91 98765 43210. Our support team is available 24/7.
