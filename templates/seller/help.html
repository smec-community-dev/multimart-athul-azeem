Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product to my store? ▼
To add a new product, go to the Products section in your dashboard and click on "Add New Product". Fill in the required details including product name, description, price, images, and inventory. Once you're done, click "Publish" to make your product live.
How can I track my order status? ▼
You can track your order status in the Orders section of your dashboard. Click on any order to see its detailed status, customer information, and shipping details. You'll also receive notifications when orders are placed, shipped, or delivered.
What payment methods do you support? ▼
ShopHub supports all major payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. Payouts to sellers are processed every 14 days.
How do I handle returns and refunds? ▼
Our standard return policy allows customers to return items within 30 days of delivery. You can customize this in your store settings. When a return is requested, you'll receive a notification and can process the refund directly from your dashboard.
How can I optimize my product listings for better visibility? ▼
Use high-quality images, write detailed descriptions with relevant keywords, set competitive prices, and encourage customer reviews. Products with complete information and positive reviews tend to rank higher in search results.
